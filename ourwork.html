Our Work

Kryige High School was started in the year 1975 by the Catholic Holy Mission Society. It is as a result, a Christian school which prioritizes in the spiritual development of the student coupled up by the academic growth. We take a keen interest in ecouraging our students to develop a personal relation with the teachers. That way, we have developed a home learning environment away from home.

We are a boys boarding High school. We believe in the equality of both genders. We have a keen interest in ensuring the social being in a student is established. As such, we take great pride at hosting various events in which our students interract with students from other schools which share our core values. Displine is core to our philosophy of life. We understand that without it, eductaion can not be achieved. We aspire to produce national frontiers and global leaders in business, education, politics among other entities.

We have a great sense of appreciating co-curriculum events. Over the years, we have participated in various national capacities in the sporting, music and drama arenas. We actually own a cabinet (very huge, by the way) full of trophies arising from our competitve performace in these events. When it comes to winning, our students are taught to be the best. This as a result prepares them for the increasingly competitive job market.
