Computer Laboratory

We are pleased to announce the commencement of the construction of a modern Computer Laboratory. It is to have a capacity to handle seventy-six (76) students in a single session. The project is expected to be complete by February 2016.

Contractor 1: Jimmy & Sons Co. Ltd

Contractor 2: Digicel Network & Internet Solutions Ltd.

Construction of a new ablusuion block

We plan to have a new ablusion block by April 2016. As such, we are opening a tendering window from 16th October 2015 through the 27th of November 2015. The ablution block is expected to serve inclusive of students with special needs. A modern architecture is needed.

All viable applications to be sent to procurement@kryige.ac.ke Bidding closes on the specified date at 1130Hrs.

Expansion of the agricultural field

Due to the increasing number of students taking Agricultural studies, it has necessitated the expansion of the existing agricultural field. This in effect should increase our capacity in handling Agriculture students' KCSE practicals. The contract is up for tendering by the 3rd of December 2015

All viable applications to be sent to procurement@kryige.ac.ke Bidding closes on the specified date at 1200Hrs.

New modern Cafeteria!

We are pleased to inform you of our ongoing construction of a modern all-inclusive food cafeteria. It has the capacity to handle 168 students at a go. The project is due in November 28th.

Contractor : Haerlord Construction company Ltd.
